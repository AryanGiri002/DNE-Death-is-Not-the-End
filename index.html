— CLASSIC MODE —
THE RECKONING OF THE DEALER
a record of what awaits you across the table
ACT I  ·  THE INTRODUCTION
Nobody knows how long the Dealer has sat at that table.
The room has no windows. The clock has no hands.
All you know is that someone slid a shotgun across the felt — and the rules are simple:

One gun. Two players. The shells decide everything.

The Dealer does not blink. The Dealer does not speak. It only watches the barrel, calculates the odds, and waits for you to make a mistake. This first level is a formality — a greeting. There are no tricks here. No gadgets. Just cold probability and nerve.
· · ·
ACT II  ·  THE ESCALATION
You survived the greeting. That means something.
The Dealer acknowledges this by reaching beneath the table.

Now there are items on the felt — tools, trinkets, chemical vices. A magnifying glass. A cigarette. Handcuffs. A handsaw.

The Dealer uses these without hesitation, without mercy. It peers through the glass. It lights up. It cuffs your wrists the moment it smells blood.

You begin to understand: this is not a fair game. It never was. The question is whether you can out-think something that was born at this table.
· · ·
ACT III  ·  THE RECKONING
The third level is called The Reckoning for a reason.

Your body has taken damage it cannot come back from — somewhere deep in your chest, beneath what the cigarettes can reach, something is fraying. The Dealer knows this. When your last real HP is gone, the cables that keep you alive get cut — no more healing, no more margin for error. One more hit and you're done.

If you win here, you walk out. The game ends. You beat Death at its own table.

But most people don't win here.
◈   D E A T H   ◈
ACT IV  ·  THE OTHER SIDE
You died in Level 3. The shell was LIVE. The lights went out.

But you didn't go anywhere.

You are still at the table — except now you are something else. A ghost. A flicker. You have no body, no HP, no heartbeat. What you have is a Ghost Orb — a fragile knot of will that refuses to fully unravel.

The Dealer is still there. Still calculating. It doesn't care that you're dead — it has seen this before. Its job now is to finish you completely, to make the death stick, to shatter the Orb before you can anchor yourself.

Your only path back is through the shells themselves.
Let three blanks wash over you. Let the nothing of them fill the Orb. And when it glows full — when the Dealer finally pulls a LIVE level on you — that bullet passes clean through your ghost and pulls you back into your body.
· · ·
ACT V  ·  RESURRECTION
You came back. Not many do.

Word travels fast on the Other Side. Every ghost who crossed over and came back empty-handed — they were watching you. They watched you absorb the blanks. They watched the Orb glow. And when you were pulled back, they felt it too, like a warmth that never reaches the living.

Now they owe you nothing, but they give you something anyway:
a whisper before each of your turns — a glimpse of the second shell in the barrel, the one after next, the one the Dealer thinks you can't see.

You defeated death once. Level 3 begins again.
This time, you are not alone at the table. This time, something from the other side is leaning over your shoulder, and it very much wants you to win.
—   G O O D   L U C K   —
LEVEL 1 — THE INTRODUCTION
HP
2 regular HP each. No faded charges. No cable-cut.
SHELLS
1–3 LIVE + 1–3 BLANK (random each load).
ITEMS
None. Pure probability. No items, no tricks.
TIP
Learn the turn loop here. If blanks outnumber lives, shooting yourself keeps your turn safely. If live shells dominate, shoot the Dealer.
WIN
Reduce Dealer HP to 0. Advance to Level 2.
LEVEL 2 — THE ESCALATION
HP
4 regular HP each. Still no faded charges.
SHELLS
2–4 LIVE + 2–4 BLANK.
ITEMS
2 items each, drawn from the full item pool.
ITEMS AVAILABLE
🔍
MAGNIFYING GLASS
Reveals the current shell in the barrel. Information only — shell stays in place.
🚬
CIGARETTE
Heal +1 regular HP. Cannot exceed max. Blocked if cables cut.
🥤
SODA
Ejects the top shell blindly — cycles to the next unknown shell. Good if you know the next is bad for you.
🔗
HANDCUFFS
Skip opponent's entire next turn. Devastating before a confirmed LIVE shell. Cannot stack.
🪚
HANDSAW
Next shot deals ×2 damage. Resets after that shot regardless of LIVE/BLANK. Combo with Magnifying Glass.
WIN
Kill the Dealer → advance to Level 3.
LEVEL 3 — THE RECKONING
HP
4 regular + 2 faded = 6 total. Faded HP cannot be healed.
CABLES
When your regular HP hits 0 (faded still remaining): CABLES CUT — healing permanently blocked, any further damage = instant death.
SHELLS
3–6 LIVE + 2–6 BLANK. Hardest shell spread.
ITEMS
4 items each from the full item pool (same 5 items as Level 2).
TIP
Protect your regular HP. Once cables are cut you can't heal — one hit ends you. Use Cigarettes early. Save Handcuffs for when Dealer is about to land a killing blow.
WIN
Kill Dealer → Game Over (Victory).
Die here in Classic mode → unlock Ghost Level 4.
GHOST LEVEL 4 — THE OTHER SIDE
ENTRY
Only accessible in Classic mode by dying in Level 3. Also playable via Ghost Mode from the main menu.
HP
You have 1 Ghost Orb. The Dealer has ∞ HP — they cannot be killed.
SHELLS
8 shells total. 2–3 are LIVE, the rest BLANK.
ORB
The Orb has two phases:

Phase 1 (0–2/3): Each BLANK shell that hits you charges the Orb +1. A LIVE shell hitting you shatters it (permanent death).

Phase 2 (3/3 — Stabilized): Rules INVERT. A LIVE shell hitting you now triggers RESURRECTION. BLANK shells have no further effect.
ITEMS
1× +4 Card each. No other items.
+4 Card: Inserts 3 BLANK + 1 LIVE at the front of the barrel (shuffled). Barrel max is 8 — excess discarded from back.
AI
Phase 1: Dealer shoots you whenever it thinks LIVE is likely (kill the Orb). Shoots itself to avoid charging your Orb with blanks.
Phase 2: Dealer always shoots itself — has ∞ HP so self-shots are free, but shooting you would trigger your resurrection.
WIN
Get hit by LIVE after Orb stabilizes → RESURRECTION → replay Level 3 with a Ghost Whisper ally (reveals 2nd shell each turn).
CORE RULES
● LIVE hits opponent → damage dealt, turn passes to them.
● LIVE hits self → damage to you, turn passes to opponent.
○ BLANK hits opponent → no damage, turn passes to them.
○ BLANK hits self → no damage, you keep your turn and shoot again.
CUFFED = entire turn skipped. Consumed after skip.
Barrel empty mid-level → reload with same level config. No new items.
Winning Level 3 (Classic) → immediate victory. No Level 4.
Dying in Level 3 (Classic) → Ghost Level 4 unlocked.
